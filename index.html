First time? macOS may block the app

Since Yapper isn't notarized with Apple, macOS may prevent it from opening. After downloading, open Terminal and run:

xattr -cr /Applications/Yapper.app

Or install directly via Terminal (no Gatekeeper):

curl -L https://github.com/yapper/yapper/releases/latest/download/Yapper-apple-silicon.dmg -o /tmp/Yapper.dmg && hdiutil attach /tmp/Yapper.dmg && cp -R "/Volumes/Yapper/Yapper.app" /Applications/ && hdiutil detach "/Volumes/Yapper" && rm /tmp/Yapper.dmg

How it works

Whisper handles transcription on your Mac. An LLM cleans it up if you want.

Nothing leaves your Mac

Whisper transcribes locally. Your audio stays on disk. Cloud AI is opt-in, not the default.

Whisper, locally

Runs OpenAI's Whisper models right on your machine. Multiple sizes from tiny to large, pick what fits.

Optional AI rewrite

Send the transcript through GPT, Claude, or a local Ollama model to clean up tone, grammar, formatting.

Reads the room

Knows your clipboard, selected text, and what app you're in. The AI uses that context to write better.

One hotkey, any app

Option+Space from anywhere. Talk, stop, text shows up at your cursor. That's the whole workflow.

Modes for everything

Email tone, casual messages, meeting notes, code comments. Six built-in, build your own on top.

⌥ + Space
